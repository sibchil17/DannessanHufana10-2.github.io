"Paper Towns"

Agloe, New York, is where part of John Green's fictional book turned movie "Paper Towns" is set. But Agloe is also a real place in upstate New York- a real-life paper town. Margo Roth Spiegelman, one of "Paper Towns" main characters, runs away to Agloe right before her high school graduation in an attempt to find herself. She glorifies the small, secluded location as an escape from her own suburban Florida hometown. To Margo, Agoe is a chance to explore the unkown.

"Twilight Saga"

If you are true Twilight fan, you can still learn a thing or two with these top ten facts about Twilight Saga.

  1. The Twilight Saga is a series of books written by Stephanie Meyer.
  2. The books in the saga are: Twilight New Moon, Eclipse, Breaking Dawn and a separate novella, The Short Second Life of Bree Tanner.
  3. The series begins Twilight, in which we meet Bella as he moves to Fork and meets vampire family, the Cullens. Falling for Edward, Bella's life comes under threat from sadistic vampire, James. Tge novel closes after an attempt on Bella's life, whereupon Bella and Edward are reunited.
  4. In New Moon, Edward leaves to protect Bella. Bella becomes withdrawn, until she becomes friends with newly turned werewolf, Jacob Black, who falls in love her. After an accident, Edward believes Bella to be dead, and travels to vampire family, the Volturi, hoping they will kill him. Bella and Edward are finally reunited, and leave with the promise to turn her into a vampire.
  5. Ecilpse opens with a string of vampire attacts from previous character, Victoria. Edward and Jacob must work together to protect Bella, their families and homes, while still dying for for her heart. Bella finally chooses to stay with Edward and agrees to marry him, to Jacob's disappointment.
  6. The Short Second Life of Bree Tanner is a novella that runs parallel to the Ecilpse plot. It follows the new born vampire Bree as her group sets out to attact Bella, Edward and Jacob.
  7. The series ends with Breaking Dawn, in which Bella and Edward get married and fall prenant. After nearly dying, Bella is turned into a vampire and their dauther Renesmee to be a danger to society, to Volturi travels to kill her. When they see otherwise, they let her live.
  8. The idea for twilight came from a dream in which a male vampire thirsted for a young girls blood.
  9. The town of Forks, Washington has become a mecca for twilight fans.
  10. The Bella-Edward-Jacob love triangle is typical of the fantasy genre, where a woman falls for a supernatural creature or is forced to choose between the two species.

"Percy Jackson"

Percy Jackson & the Olympians, often shortened to Percy Jackson is a pentalogy of fantasy adventure novels written by American author Rick Riordan (also called "Uncle Rick" by his fans and readers), and the first book series in the Camp Half-Blood Chronicles. Five supplementary books, along with three graphic novels, have also been released. More than 45 million coplies of the books have been sold in more than 35 Countries.
