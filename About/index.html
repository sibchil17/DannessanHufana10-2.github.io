"Books Series"

Twilight is a series of four vampire-themed fantasy romance novels by American author Stephanie Meyer. The four books chart the later teen year of Isabella "Bella" Swan, a girl who moves to Forks, Washington, and falls in love with a 104 years old vampire named Edward Cullen. The series is told primarily from Bella's point of view, with the epilouge of Eclipse and Part 2 of Breaking Dawn being told from a viewpoint of character Jacob Black, a werewolf. The unpublished Midnight Sun is a retelling of the first book, Twilight, from Edward Cullen's point of view. "Book Series"

The Lightning Thief(book 1) is an American fantasy-adventure novel based on Greek Mythology, the first young novel written by Rick Riordan in the Percy Jackson & the Olymians series. The novel charts the adventures of modern-day 12 years old Percy Jackson as he discovers he is a demigod, the son of a mortal woman and the Greek God Poseidon. The Sea Monsters(book 2) is a second novel in Percy Jackson & the Olymians series and the sequel to The Lightning Thief. This book chronicles the adventures of 13 years old demigod, Percy Jackson as he and his friends rescue his satyr friend Grover from the Cyclops Polyphemus and save the camp from a Titan's attack by bringing the Golden Fleece to cure Thalia's poisoned pine tree. The Titan's Cure(book 3) is the third novel in the Percy Jackson & the Olymians series and the sequel to the The Sea of Monsters. It is about the adventures of a 14 years old demigod Percy Jackson as he and his friends go on a dangerous quest to rescue his 14 years old demigod friend Annabeth Chase and the Greek Goddess Artemis, who both been kidnaped.
